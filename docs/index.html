Welcome to Pricing Pal!

What is Pricing Pal?

Pricing Pal is an Android application built for non-profit thrift stores, although it may be useful to others too! We offer cloud storage and quick editing of basic pricing lists, so that everyone who works in your store can have easy access to your up-to-date pricing list -- no sign-up required. You, as a registered admin user, can create and edit your own pricing lists, in-app, from anywhere with an Internet collection. This is a new project made by a team of students at Georgia Gwinnett College, and we hope you find it useful!
